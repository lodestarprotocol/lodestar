Why no-liquidation wins on Flare

Only the calendar defaults you

No health factor, no margin calls. XRP can wick 60% at 3am — your collateral is untouched. Miss the deadline and you still receive the surplus.

FTSOv2 native oracle

Prices come from Flare's enshrined oracle — the feed securing the chain — not a flash-loanable DEX spot.

Collateral keeps earning

Locked sFLR and stXRP keep earning their staking yield for the whole term — that appreciation returns to you on repay.
